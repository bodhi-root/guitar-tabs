Boulevard of Broken Dreams

This is a nice, medium-paced strumming song. You can play it with a capo and strum the familiar chords or play it with barred chords (see second tab section below).

Chorus

Lyrics and Simple Version

If you look at the next tab (more complicated) it suggests playing the verses using barred chords and the chorus using power chords.
