'STAIRWAY TO HEAVEN' - (continued)


The FIRST PART begins without words,
playing the first four lines until the
repeat and then the verse begins:


FIRST PART:

There's a lady who's sure all that glitters is gold,
And she's buying a stairway to heaven.
When she gets there she knows, if the stores are all closed,
With a word she can get what she came for.

Oooh, oooh, ooh-ooh-ooh, oooh,
And she's buying a stairway to heaven.
There's a sign on the wall, but she wants to be sure,
'Cause you know sometimes words have two meanings.

In a tree by the brook there's a songbird who sings.
Sometimes all of our thoughts are misgiven.


    'Ooh, and it makes me wonder' is sung over

    [INTERLUDE bit and in the SECOND PART you
     play the VERSE and then the INTERLUDE and
     repeat the two again and again.]


SECOND PART:

There's a feeling I get when I look to the west,
And my spirit is crying for leaving.
In my thoughts I have seen rings of smoke through the trees
And the voices of those who stand looking.

And it's whispered that soon, if we all call the tune,
Then the Piper will lead us to reason.
And a new day will dawn for those who stand long,
And the forests will echo with laughter.

If there's a bustle in your hedgerow don't be alarmed now.
It's just a spring clean for the May Queen.
Yes there are two paths you can go by, but in the long run,
There's still time to change the road you're on.

Your head is humming and it won't go - in case you don't know
The Piper's calling you to join him.
Dear lady can you hear the wind blow, and did you know
Your stairway lies on the whispering wind?



    [Then after the TRANSITION AND SOLO
     the THIRD PART comes in with
     renewed energy.]


THIRD PART:

And as we wind on down the road
Our shadows taller than our soul
There walks a lady we all know
Who shines with light and wants to show
How everything still turns to gold
And if you listen very hard
The tune will come to you at last
When all are on and one is all
To be a rock and not a roll!
