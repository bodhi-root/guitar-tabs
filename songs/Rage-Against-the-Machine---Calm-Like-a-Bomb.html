In this song Tom use a Whammy pedal set 2 octaves up and a delay pedal for the solo

WHAMMY PEDAL:

+  close whammy
*  open whammy
>  gradually open whammy
<  gradually release whammy

DELAY PEDAL:

5% feedback
40 or 50 level
about 40~45 ms
