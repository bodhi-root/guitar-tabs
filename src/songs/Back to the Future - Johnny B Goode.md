## Intro

There's a good video on how Chuck Berry plays this:

* https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zg6t5LPjc6k

The intro tab below is from this video.  The video also emphasizes some interesting points:

* The song is based around a Bb7 (```|686766|```)
* The addition of "Blue notes" (a flat 7th and flat 5th) with a major chord give it that "bluesy" feel
* Mixolydian scale used over Bb7 (good scale for solo-ing over blues). Also flat 7th.
